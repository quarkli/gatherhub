About us

We are humble creators and developers who wants to contribute our skill and expertise to help our society. We believe an open, equal, and convenient communication tool which can be easily accessed by anyone is essential to the human society development. People should be free to choose any location to live while meantime still have equal opportunity to get fine jobs and education. Hence, we created Gatherhub. A open square and tool on the Internet to help people overcome the barrier of space and being able to collaborate together in real time.

Our mission is targeting to create the greatest value for the human society than the commercial profit. We hope the application we created is easy to use for everyone. Therefore, we get rid of any form of methods to collect user's information or trying to create any loyalty. There is no user account creation, no complex application forms, no login validations. Gatherhub is provided to people who needs it and nothing more. We are not trying to sell it to everybody. We only focus on improving the technology itself and sincerely believe the greater reward will come to us when we made it right.

If you share the same vision with us and want to be part of it, we welcome you to join us and become our force to accelerate the development. We accept donation to fund our infrastructure and operation cost. And we also welcome developers to join us to improve this open platform. We want Gatherhub becomes an Internet open square of the people, by the people, and for the people.
